Privacy Policy

Bible Answers · Last updated: March 10, 2026

1. What we collect

Bible Answers does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information.

When you ask a question, the text of your question is sent to OpenAI's API to generate a Biblical answer. OpenAI may process this data according to their own privacy policy at openai.com/privacy.

2. How your data is stored

Your questions, answers, and app settings (including subscription status) are stored locally on your device using iOS Secure Storage. This data never leaves your device except for the question text sent to OpenAI.

3. AI & OpenAI

Bible Answers uses artificial intelligence (OpenAI GPT-4o) to generate Biblical guidance. The questions you ask are sent to OpenAI's servers to generate responses. We do not store your questions on our servers. Please do not include sensitive personal information in your questions.

4. Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID settings.

5. Push notifications

If you grant permission, we may send you daily inspirational notifications. You can disable these at any time in your iPhone Settings.

6. Children

Bible Answers is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: bibleanswersapp@gmail.com
